As noted earlier, most refer to HTML "elements" as "tags" as shorthand. eg. "header element" and "header tag" are interchangeable Here are a few common HTML elements, there are approximately 110 different HTML elements/tags!

Header

the header will be the title of the website

the number signifies the size of the header, 1 being the largest and 5 the smallest

Nav

the nav's purpose is to provide navigation links for the site

usually positioned at the top of the page

Paragraph

This is where the body of the text will go

not as simple as pressing return, you must create a new paragraph tag for each section of text
